My Impressions

  • GPT-5 tends to favor dark themes, producing smooth animations and seamless page transitions. The overall look is sleek and professional, resembling high-end photo editing software.
  • Grok 4 (Cursor) delivers strong performance in coding the business logic, but its UI design feels unrefined. It gives the impression of being built by a skilled developer with limited attention to visual design principles.
  • In comparing Opus 4.1 and Sonnet 4, the Opus 4.1 output appears more polished and sophisticated, whereas the Sonnet design comes across as simpler and less refined.
